The Unbreakable Dream

Every great dream begins with a dreamer. Ayuush’s story is not about perfection — it’s about persistence. When life threw challenges, he didn’t give up. Broken bones, blurry vision, yet a heart that never stopped believing. Each setback became a lesson, each scar a symbol of strength.

His journey reminds us: being unbreakable doesn’t mean you never fall. It means you rise every single time, stronger, wiser, and unstoppable. The dream continues — and so does the fight to make it real.
